Licensed Moneylenders Registered In Singapore

Important Note: Licensed moneylenders are disallowed to solicit for loans via whatsapp, text messages or phone calls. If you have received such a message or phone call, it is likely that an unlicensed moneylender (a.k.a. loan shark) has approached you, or more likely a loan scam.

Do not respond to the message or call. Report such activities to the Police hotline at 1800-255-000, or submit the information online at www.police.gov.sg/iwitness. Members of the public may also call the National Crime Prevention Council’s X-Ah Long hotline at 1800-924-5664.

How much can you borrow

For secured loans, you can obtain a loan of any amount. For unsecured loans, you can obtain:

For Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents (PR)

Up to $3,000, if your annual income is less than $20,000, and up to 6 times your monthly income, if your annual income is $20,000 and above.

For Foreigners residing in Singapore

Up to $500, if your annual income is less than $10,000, up to $3,000, if your annual income is at least $10,000, but less than $20,000, and up to 6 times your monthly income, if your annual income is $20,000 and above.
